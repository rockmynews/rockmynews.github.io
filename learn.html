Professor Sudheer Bhaskar part 1

How to surrender to Swami?
Play at 24: 50

“In fact once Swami told out of one incident that happened on 1991, not a pleasant one. Swami said I was physically present there when this event was happening in your life. It had to happen. That’s why I did not intervene.”

“On another occasion Swami was interacting with us. Swami said that look please understand, once you surrender to Me, I will never allow anything wrong to happen to you. Please take it whatever happens is good for your life. Only good for your life.”

“Once Swami also said… even in your dream, even by mistake, you say once, Swami I’m yours. You are mine. And after making this statement he says sorry sorry Swami I made a mistake I want to withdraw this statement. Swami says, once it has come out of your lips, I will hold on to you for rest of the time. You are under me. You cannot be out of me.”
