Not investing in Gold

Long time ago I asked to a person what inflation was. I got a good answer; inflation is increasing of price while the value of something is same. I also learnt that the person was buying gold and silver; kind of like investing. I thought that wasn't great because stocks offer great return if done nicely. The reasoning was that the price of gold always increases which is safe and great.

Some time ago I was listening to people talk about the value of money decades ago and what it could buy. It made me think if gold was really a good 'investment'. I realised that on the surface the price of gold increases, actually price of everything increases. To understand if very long term investment in gold is worth it, we need to compare it with something.

Current year: 2015

1 ounce gold = $100 . This is what you bought for a very long term investment.

1 pound coca = $10 . This is what it costs in the market if you want to buy.

You can buy 10 pounds of coca with $100

10 years later: 2025.

Price of gold has increased 20%.

Price of coca has increased 10%.

Your $100 investment in gold has become worth $120. Not that bad.

The price of coca has increased to $11. Not great but you can afford.

How much coca can you buy with your $120 dollars now?

1 pound coca = $11

You have $120

You can buy 10.9 pounds of coca with $120. Not even 11 pounds, but almost.

How would you feel if your 10 year investment could not even buy you 1 more pound of coca? This is the issue, people tend to look only at what they like. They forget that there is an environment around it and many factors to influence it, good or bad. Your investment becomes more valuable but so does the shampoo and your toothbrush.

There are people however who must be investing in gold and earning good money. The difference between you and the person is that people has researched about it and knows way more about not just gold, but factors around gold. You on the other hand are busy spending days researching which oven is better while dumping money without knowledge in gold, calling if safe.

There are things all around. Good and bad. High and lows. We get to choose. And we do.
