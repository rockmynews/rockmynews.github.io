It is not there

Love happens in the mind. Why does one love another? I do not know. But it does happen in the mind. Once the memory is lost, one does not know to love anyone. It becomes a clean slate.

A great many deal of things are required to love.

Trust, faith, tolerance, respect, patience, understanding, observation. I do not know to put love as a requirement of love. I really do not know. What comes first. What comes late. Only time tells. If it does tell.

Do I know you? What do I mean by know?

Could I tell why you are angry? Could I tell that you will be angry?

Maybe that is knowing.

What is it to be a grown up? Or small?

Time? Let us say mind. For that is what changes over time.

If one slept for 10 years and woke up, would they have grown by 10 years?

Physically, sure. But growing necessarily isn't about what is seen.

It being invisible must make it difficult to know. For one cannot see, one cannot tell.

One might accept, or reject. Live. Or leave.

How does one do it?

In the mind?

Heart?
