Just Start

We all tend to think of things but never start. That is just us. Starting takes a lot of energy. When a rocket is launched, most of the fuel is used in the first few minutes. The beginning seems far and difficult.

But here it is why you should start. Right now you have not started. It means zero(0). Tomorrow when you start even a bit, how big will that be? Even if you went 1 step towards it, it will be a lot. In fact it willbe so much you will not be able to calculate.

1⁄10 x 100 = ∞

That will make all the difference.

Therefore, just Start.
